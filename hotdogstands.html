The hot dog stand (pølsevogn) is a Danish institution, frequented by everyone.
Hot dogs are the Danish street food and have been eaten in Aarhus since 1917, sold from wagons.

There are many different ones but you’ll find Børnenes Kontor to be special. Why?
They are run by a humanitarian organisation, that gives the profit of the fastfood
sales to underprivileged children and families.

In Aarhus, you’re never too far from a pølsevogn, but do go and find one
owned by Børnenes Kiosk - your hot dog will taste all the better for
helping out those in need.
