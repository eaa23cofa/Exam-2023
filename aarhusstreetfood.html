This former bus garage now houses 30 very different stalls with a wide range of food and drinks.

There are burgers with duck confit, Vietnamese bánh mì, rolex and sweet potato fries from Uganda,
Breton galettes, gourmet toast with grilled cheese, falafels in pita bread, vegan Indian rajma,
American wings, hummus from the Middle east, Thai rolls, Chinese bao buns, Mexican tacos
and fajitas, fish and chips, pizzas and pasta dishes - to mention just a few examples.

Some 450 people from 38 nations work at Aarhus Street Food, so a visit here really is a
culinary trip around the world.
